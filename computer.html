Introduction

This page concentrates on computer systems and they work. At the end you will understand how computers operate and should be able to use I.C.T (Information and Communications Technologies) to perform a variety of everyday tasks.

In particular, you will be able to:

  • describe and use hardware.
  • use the operating environment.
  • identify, describe and use systems applications and tools.
