Do you want to learn to program?

Would you like to develop websites or apps but you lack skills?

Then you are at your place at TechTeach. During our TechTeach sessions, knowledge is passed on at different levels and on various topics in I.T. Accessibility and diversity are paramount: everyone with any background is welcome!

At TechTeach we share our programming knowledge. If you are interested in learning a specific programming language, creating a website or developing applications, you are welcome for the TechTeach sessions. Each participant can work on what interests him most and it is not necessary to have knowledge about programming in advance.

Professional TechTeachers offer support where necessary, and there is also the possibility as a student to grow into a TechTeacher. This accessible way of learning makes it possible for everyone to learn or learn something about software development. Working as a volunteer at TechTeach? Even then you are welcome with us! TechTeach is a non-profit organization with the aim of increasing diversity in the I.T sector.

During the sessions each person works individually and when necessary receives help from a present TechTeacher. TechTeachers are not teachers, but they have knowledge of the subject, and in that way they will be able to assist the participants. TechTeachers can also help in deciding which topic would be the most interesting for the students. Participation in the sessions will always be free.

If you're want to learn Programming or Web Developing but you don't have any knowledge or skills on computer, don't worry, fear or be ashamed it's normal since people born when they know nothing but we just all learn.
TechTeach Uganda provides the Basic I.T course for people who are just start learning computer, so we can start with learning this course and when you acquire the computer basic skills then you can proceed with learning what you like.

Philosophy

TechTeach is a place where we all learn; the participants and the TechTeachers as well. We want it to be a safe environment and where everyone feels welcome.

Learning programming will not always be easy and you will make mistakes; that's normal, and it's just a sign that you're learning something new. Discussing a problem you encounter with other students or TechTeachers can help you think of new solutions.

TechTeach International

TechTeach is an international concept! There are also TechTeach sessions in Deventer, Holland, Europe. We started in Deventer in December 2017 and the participants learned about different topics such as Java, create a website and Python.
Find the main page that is in Dutch and English Here.

There is also TechTeach Esteli, here the sessions are taking place in Esteli, Nicaragua-Central America. This was established in 2018 and participants learning Web Development and Designing, Java Programming and Arduino UNO. For the case of kids they learn how to build games, animations e.t.c using Scratch. It is a programming language for children, easy and fun to learn. Hope to start in Uganda ASAP.
